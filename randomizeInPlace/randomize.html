PSEUDOCODE FOR RANDOMIZE IN PLACE

Goal: Randomize an array A of length n

RANDOMIZE-IN-PLACE(A)
  1. n = A.length
  2. for i = 1 to n
  3.     swap A[i] with A[Random(i,n)]   (CLICK SWAP)
